The Portfolio of Adam Gunn

Projects

  • Metroid (Unity Remaster)
    • I developed the enemies, as well as the zoomer powerup mechanic. The enemies included all of those in the first area (Zoomers, Zebs, Reos, Skrees, and Rippers). The powerup mechanic essentially allows the player to turn into one of the zoomers, enabling them to crawl on walls and ceilings. I also developed the player and enemy animations. I used Unity with C#.
  • Polevault
    • A 2D platformer with no jumps. Instead of jumping, players traverse levels by toggling magnetic blocks that can either attract or repel the player. Later in the game, players unlock the ability to place their own magnets, opening up the game for further problem-solving. I developed this game solely by myself (using some external assets), over the course of 2 weeks.
